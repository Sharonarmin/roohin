Our Cold Rooms & Refrigeration Solutions

Every cold room solution is engineered to your exact specifications — designed for the UAE climate, compliant with local standards, and built for lasting performance.

01

Walk-In Chiller Room

Maintain consistent chilled temperatures to preserve freshness, quality, and food safety. Our walk-in chiller rooms are custom-designed to match your space, storage volume, and operational workflow. Built with high-grade insulation and energy-efficient systems, they ensure reliable performance even in demanding UAE conditions. Ideal for restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and commercial kitchens.

02

Walk-In Freezer Room

Engineered for stable sub-zero temperatures, our freezer rooms protect frozen goods without temperature fluctuations. We design each unit based on your product type, usage frequency, and capacity needs. Durable panels, airtight sealing, and efficient refrigeration deliver long-term reliability and reduced energy costs. Perfect for meat, seafood, frozen food storage, and distribution operations.

Stable sub-zero temperature control
Airtight sealing & durable panels
Reduced energy costs long-term
03

Blast Freezer & Blast Chiller

Rapid cooling technology designed to quickly reduce product temperature while preserving taste, texture, and safety. Our blast systems help extend shelf life, improve hygiene compliance, and support high-volume kitchen or production environments. Configured to your workflow and output requirements, they ensure fast, uniform cooling cycles. Essential for bakeries, hotels, catering facilities, and food production units.

Extends shelf life & hygiene compliance
Fast, uniform cooling cycles
